Recipe #1:Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour
Baking powder
Sugar
Milk
Egg
Butter (melted)		 1 cup
1 tbsp
2 tbsp
3/4 cup
1
2 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk milk, egg, and butter in a separate bowl.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients, and whisk until smooth.
  4. Pour batter onto a hot, greased skillet and cook until bubbles appear. Flip and cook the other side.
